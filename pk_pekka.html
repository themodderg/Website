Behavior

The P.E.K.K.A. is a tamed melee bodyguard. It follows its owner, sits on command and retaliates against anything that attacks it or its owner. It ignores fall damage and cannot be knocked back.

Attacks land in a 1.45×2.00×1.5 hitbox in front of the golem and deal 30 per swing. Under the Rage effect its attack speed goes up by 30%.

Spawning

P.E.K.K.A.s do not spawn naturally. They are built by hand from four Iron Blocks arranged in the iron-golem T-shape, capped with a P.E.K.K.A. Head. The golem spawns tamed to whoever placed the head.

Building

Build the iron-golem shape (one iron block body, one iron block on top, one iron block on each side for arms) and place the P.E.K.K.A. Head where a carved pumpkin would go. The blocks are consumed and the golem drops in with an enchant burst and spawn sound.

  • P.E.K.K.A. Head (1)
  • Iron Block (4, iron-golem shape)

Interactions

Empty hand (right-click): cycles through Follow, Sit and Wander command modes.

Iron Ingot (right-click): heals the golem for 3 and consumes one ingot, up to full health.

Star Item (right-click): promotes the P.E.K.K.A. to Star level once it has scored 50 kills, unlocking the golden crown look.

Evo Crystal (right-click): transforms the P.E.K.K.A. into its Evolved form. In Evo mode every kill spawns a Butterfly that flies back to the golem and heals it for 3.5% to 15% of its max health depending on the victim's health pool, banking overflow as absorption up to half its max.

Drops

On death the P.E.K.K.A. always drops a P.E.K.K.A. Head.

  • P.E.K.K.A. Head (1)
