Mob Drops

Dropped 1 for 1 when a P.E.K.K.A. dies. The drop is unconditional and ignores looting.

Usage

The head is the crown block for the P.E.K.K.A. summon. Build the iron-golem T-shape out of four Iron Blocks and place a P.E.K.K.A. Head where the carved pumpkin would go. The blocks are consumed and the P.E.K.K.A. spawns tamed to whoever placed the head. It also works as a decorative mob head placed on the floor or the wall.
