Tipped Darts

Fill a crafting grid with eight Darts around any Potion in the center to produce eight Tipped Darts that inherit the potion's effects. Works with regular, splash and lingering potion inputs — the resulting darts apply the potion's effect on hit.

8
  • Dart
  • Potion (any)

Usage

Darts are fired by the Blowgun. Keep a stack in your inventory while holding the blowgun to shoot them at targets. Tipped darts, crafted with a potion, deal the potion's effect to whatever they hit.
