Crafting

Shapeless recipe: the six fragments can go in any six slots.

Usage

Right-click a tamed P.E.K.K.A. to consume the crystal and transform it into its Evolved form. Evolved P.E.K.K.A.s gain purple armor plating, a faster attack tempo, and heal themselves from every kill via a butterfly that carries the drained life back to the golem.
