Behavior

Castle Golems are the heaviest defenders in the mod: 240 HP, 28 attack damage, and knockback immunity, wrapped around a wide slam that leaves dirt and campfire smoke particles on impact. They wander their village at a slow 0.20 speed, ignore Creepers, and only turn on players who attack them first.

They cannot be pushed. Anything that walks into their AoE gets nudged aside by their own doPush.

Castle Mode

The Castle Golem's signature ability is Castle Mode: it folds up into a small stone fortress and roots itself in place. When it spawns naturally in the world it wakes up already in castle form.

While castled, it beats a slow heartbeat sound every second and pulses a 50 block aura that applies Mining Fatigue III for 5 seconds to every non-owner, non-creative player in range. The aura refreshes every 2 seconds, so griefers around a village are stuck at that fatigue level for as long as they stay close.

The castle scans a 15 block radius for hostile mobs every half second. Any enemy inside that ring, or a hit taken from a player, unfolds it back into its mobile form so it can chase.

Spawning

Castle Golems appear in two ways: they replace roughly one in three Iron Golem spawns inside Plains and Sunflower Plains villages, and they can be built by hand from a small castle-shaped structure crowned with a Carved Pumpkin.

Natural Spawning

When a village attempts to spawn an Iron Golem in Plains or Sunflower Plains, the mod rolls a 1-in-3 chance to swap it for a Castle Golem. The natural spawn drops it into the world already in castle form, so villages get a stationary keep as their default defender.

Building

The build stack is a small castle. From the top: a Brick above a Carved Pumpkin (or Jack o'Lantern), two Clay blocks and a Diamond Block below to form the main keep. Two side towers 2 blocks out on either side (north/south or east/west) each need one Brick over two Clay blocks. The gap between each side tower and the keep needs one Clay block at the base to connect them.

The last block placed must be either the Carved Pumpkin on top of the keep column, or a Brick placed directly above that pumpkin. When the pattern matches, every block above is consumed and the golem spawns owned by the player who placed the trigger block.

Elevation view: keep column in the centre, side towers two blocks out, connectors at the base.

  • Carved Pumpkin or Jack o'Lantern (1)
  • Bricks (3)
  • Clay (8)
  • Diamond Block (1)

Interactions

Diamond (right-click): heals the golem for 40 and consumes one diamond, up to full health.

Empty hand (right-click, owner only): toggles Castle Mode. From the mobile form it folds into the castle over 83 ticks; from the castle it unfolds over 109 ticks. Non-owners get a "Not your Golem!" hotbar message and cannot toggle it. Creative players can toggle any Castle Golem regardless of ownership.

Drops

On death the golem drops 3 to 6 Bricks and 1 to 3 Diamonds.

  • Bricks (3–6)
  • Diamond (1–3)
