About The Mod

PEKKA Craft brings the Mini P.E.K.K.A. and P.E.K.K.A. from Clash Royale into Minecraft, along with their evolved and hero variants, dedicated gear, pancakes, and a small crystal-based upgrade economy sourced from dungeons.
