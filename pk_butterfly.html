Behavior

The Butterfly is a small non-living entity, not a mob: it has no health or attack, cannot be attacked or targeted, and passes through blocks and other entities (no physics). It exists purely to carry a lifesteal payload from a kill back to the Evolved P.E.K.K.A. that earned it.

On spawn it rises straight up for 15 ticks, then hovers in place with a gentle up-and-down bob for another 25 ticks, then flies directly toward its Evolved P.E.K.K.A. When it reaches the golem, it delivers its stored heal and immediately despawns. If its target dies or is unloaded before the Butterfly arrives, it despawns without effect.

Spawning

A Butterfly cannot be summoned, crafted or built. One spawns automatically at the position of any creature killed by an Evolved P.E.K.K.A., carrying a heal worth 3.5% to 15% of the golem's max health depending on the victim's own max health (scaling between 6 and 100 HP victims).
