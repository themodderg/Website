Usage

Sword-tier weapon on the Gold tier. Deals 4 per hit at 2 attacks per second, so the DPS is comparable to a diamond sword despite the paper-thin durability.

Killing any living enemy with the Spatula heals the wielder for 2 and, on a 10-second cooldown per weapon, plays the pancakes jingle.

The weapon is also carried by Hero P.E.K.K.A., where it becomes part of the Hero attack cycle.
