Usage

Eaten normally for 6 hunger and 7.2 saturation.

Right-click a tamed Mini P.E.K.K.A. with a pancake to heal it for 2 . If the Mini is in Hero form and its charge bar is full, the pancake instead triggers the Hero ability, which heals for 40% of max HP, adds 50% attack damage for 30 seconds and grants a short invulnerability window.

Right-click a Mini P.E.K.K.A. Head placed on an Iron Block to summon a fresh Mini P.E.K.K.A., tamed to whoever fed it the pancake.
