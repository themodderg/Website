Behavior

Hero Mini P.E.K.K.A. is not a separate mob. It is the Mini P.E.K.K.A. entity switched into its Hero form, and it keeps the same base health, attack and command behavior (Follow, Sit, Wander). The transformation only swaps its model, sounds and combo, and unlocks a charge-and-release ability.

Every hit that lands while in Hero form fills one point of a 6-point charge meter. Attacks alternate between two swings on a 2-hit combo, an 18-tick swing and a slower 23-tick swing, instead of the base Mini's single attack pattern.

Spawning

Hero Mini P.E.K.K.A. cannot be built or found directly. Right-click a tamed Mini P.E.K.K.A. with a Hero Crystal to switch it into Hero form permanently; the crystal is consumed on use (outside creative).

A Hero Crystal is crafted from four Big Hero Shards around a Gold Block. Big Hero Shards are the rarest reward from opening a Hero Box, which itself drops at a 10–45% chance from dungeon, temple, nether fortress, end city, ancient city, woodland mansion, bastion and stronghold chests.

Interactions

Empty hand (right-click): cycles through Follow, Sit and Wander command modes, same as the base Mini.

Pancake (right-click): while the charge meter is below 6, heals 2 per pancake like the base Mini. Once the meter is full, feeding a Pancake instead burns the charge to trigger the Hero ability: heal 40 (40% of max health), add 50% attack damage for 30 seconds, and grant a short invulnerability window at the start of the buff.

Star Item (right-click): still available in Hero form once it has scored 50 kills, layering the golden Star look on top of Hero mode.

Rename to pancakes: swaps the Hero onto its Pancakes skin variant, same as the base Mini.

Drops

On death, Hero Mini P.E.K.K.A. always drops a Mini P.E.K.K.A. Head, same as the base Mini. The Hero transformation itself is lost on death.
