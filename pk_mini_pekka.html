Behavior

The Mini P.E.K.K.A. is a tamed melee companion. It follows its owner, sits on command and retaliates against anything that attacks it or its owner. It ignores fall damage and cannot be knocked back.

Attacks land in a 0.85×1.00×1.5 hitbox and deal 18 per swing. Under the Rage effect its attack speed goes up by 30%.

Spawning

Mini P.E.K.K.A.s do not spawn naturally. They are built by placing a Mini P.E.K.K.A. Head on an Iron Block and using a Pancake on the stack. The Mini spawns tamed to whoever fed it the pancake.

Building

Stack a Mini P.E.K.K.A. Head on top of an Iron Block, then right-click the stack with a Pancake. Both blocks are consumed, the pancake is eaten, and the Mini drops in with an anvil thud and an enchant burst.

  • Mini P.E.K.K.A. Head (1)
  • Iron Block (1)
  • Pancake (1, used on the stack)

Interactions

Empty hand (right-click): cycles through Follow, Sit and Wander command modes.

Pancake (right-click): heals the Mini for 2 and consumes one pancake. While the Hero form is charged, feeding a pancake instead triggers its ability (see below).

Star Item (right-click): promotes the Mini to Star level once it has scored 50 kills.

Hero Crystal (right-click): transforms the Mini into its Hero form. In Hero mode every hit builds one point of charge, up to 6. Once fully charged, feeding a Pancake burns the charge to heal 40 , add 50% attack damage for 30 seconds and grant a short invulnerability window at the start.

Rename to pancakes: swaps the Mini onto its Pancakes skin and plays the pancakes jingle. The custom name itself is cleared, so the skin sticks without a nameplate hanging above the golem.

Drops

On death the Mini P.E.K.K.A. always drops a Mini P.E.K.K.A. Head.

  • Mini P.E.K.K.A. Head (1)
