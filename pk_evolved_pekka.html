Behavior

Evolved P.E.K.K.A. is not a separate mob. It is the P.E.K.K.A. entity switched into its Evo form, and it keeps the same base health, attack and command behavior (Follow, Sit, Wander). The transformation swaps its model, ambient sound, step sound and attack sound, and grants a passive lifesteal ability: every kill spawns a Butterfly.

The Butterfly flies back to the Evolved P.E.K.K.A. and heals it for 3.5% to 15% of its max health, scaling with the victim's max health (interpolated between 6 and 100 HP victims). Any healing beyond missing health is banked as absorption (a shield), capped at 50% of the golem's max health, so an Evolved P.E.K.K.A. can sit above its 200 pool with up to 100 of absorption banked from kills.

Spawning

Evolved P.E.K.K.A. cannot be built or found directly. Right-click a tamed P.E.K.K.A. with an Evolution Crystal to switch it into Evo form permanently; the crystal is consumed on use (outside creative).

An Evolution Crystal is crafted shapeless from six Evo Fragments. Evo Fragments drop from the same loot pools as Hero Shards: dungeon, temple and nether fortress chests (15–30% chance, 1–3 fragments), and end city, ancient city, woodland mansion, bastion and stronghold chests (50% chance, 2–3 fragments).

Interactions

Empty hand (right-click): cycles through Follow, Sit and Wander command modes, same as the base P.E.K.K.A.

Iron Ingot (right-click): heals the golem for 3 and consumes one ingot, same as the base P.E.K.K.A.

Star Item (right-click): still available in Evo form once it has scored 50 kills, layering the golden Star look on top of Evo mode.

Rage effect: still applies its 30% attack speed boost on top of Evo mode.

Drops

On death, Evolved P.E.K.K.A. always drops a P.E.K.K.A. Head, same as the base golem. The Evo transformation itself is lost on death.
