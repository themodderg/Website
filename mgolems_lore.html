Mobs

New golem variants inspired by different biomes and structures. Each one is built from its own material set and brings its own way of defending its territory.

Items

A handful of weapons, projectiles and materials tied to the golems and the places they come from. Some drop from mobs, others are crafted from what they leave behind.
