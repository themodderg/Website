Usage

A charging melee weapon on a diamond-tier frame. Each successful hit on a living entity feeds the sword's Solaris energy, advancing it through five stages. Higher stages add a Solar Bonus to the base attack damage.

The counter climbs one hit at a time and stage transitions play a resonating chime. At the final stage, the blade is marked Maximum Overcharge: the next strike deals its full solar payload, sets the target on fire, and discharges the sword back to stage 1.

Stages

Stage progression is stored on the item itself and persists across worlds. Hits required and bonus damage per stage:

  • Stage 1: base sword, +0 solar bonus
  • Stage 2: reached after 1 hit, +2 solar bonus
  • Stage 3: reached after 2 hits, +4 solar bonus
  • Stage 4: reached after 3 hits, +6 solar bonus
  • Stage 5: Maximum Overcharge, next hit deals +8 solar and ignites the target, then resets to stage 1
