Chest Loot

Injected into vanilla chest loot tables via loot modifiers. Drop counts and chances scale with the tier of the chest.

Tier I (15%, 1–2 fragments): abandoned mineshaft, shipwreck treasure, ruined portal, pillager outpost.

Tier II (30%, 1–3 fragments): simple dungeon, desert pyramid, jungle temple, nether fortress.

Tier III (50%, 2–3 fragments): end city treasure, ancient city, woodland mansion, bastion treasure, stronghold library.

Usage

Six Evo Fragments combine in a shapeless recipe into one Evolution Crystal, which unlocks the Evolved form of the P.E.K.K.A.
